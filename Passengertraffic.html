The Immigrants Journey

 

The majority of Swedish emigrants after late 1880's travelled by boat from Göteborg to Hull and then by train to Liverpool
from which the journey continued with the big transatlantic passenger steamers. This is the route that my great grand-father, Nils P. Falkman
took when he left Sweden. Headed to America.  My great-grandmother on the other hand, travelled from Arbrä, to Göteborg. And then boarded
a boat to Hamburg, Germany. And from Homburg, by train to Bremen. And it was from Bremen that she sailed to America.
