The majority of Swedish emigrants after late 1880's traveled by boat from Göteborg to Hull and then by train to Liverpool from which the journey continued with the big transatlantic passenger steamers. This is the route that my great grand-father, Nils P. Falkman took when he left Sweden. Headed to America. My great-grandmother on the other hand, traveled from Arbrä (not shown), to Göteborg. From there she boarded a boat to Hamburg, Germany, and from Hamburg traveled by train to Bremen. And it was from Bremen that she sailed to America.
